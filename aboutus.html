About Us

Building healthier communities through sport, movement and inclusion

We Are Proud To Be Not-For-Profit

Life hope Limited, trading as Badminton boys, badminton junior, power hitters, Chelmsford tiger, cycling club and Chess club, is a genuine not-for-profit organisation with HMRC. We have no shareholders and, as an exempt charity, we re-invest all surpluses back into the services we operate so we can provide the best possible experience for our communities.

Everything we do is shaped by service, access and long-term value for the people who use our clubs and centres. That means better sessions, stronger facilities, more welcoming environments and more opportunities for children, families and adults to stay active together.

We Care For The Health Of Our Communities

We want to support the health of the nation by enabling people to live longer, happier and healthier lives. As a not-for-profit charitable leisure trust, supporting individuals and communities to be more active and healthier is at the heart of everything we do.

We believe in health at all ages. We want the local communities we serve to have fun, feel welcome, be included and feel empowered to live healthy lives through regular activity, sport and positive social connection.

Our Partnerships

In the areas of sport, health and community development, we are committed to working in partnership with, or acting as an agent on behalf of, local authorities and other local organisations.

Our goal is to help deliver a shared community and economic vision centred around people, access, wellbeing and strong local relationships.

Activities And Facilities

Swimming

Swimming is a healthy activity that can be enjoyed by people of any age and ability. It offers a wide range of health benefits and can be enjoyed alone, with friends and family, or as part of a group exercise routine.

Indoor & Outdoor Sports

Whether you are booking a badminton court with a partner or organising a fixture for a team or group, we provide well-maintained, high-quality facilities in your local area.

Racket Sports

Playing badminton, squash or tennis is not only fun, it also improves coordination, balance and general mobility while helping to strengthen your arms and legs.

Flexible Community Bookings

Our centres also offer courts and pitches for teams and groups to hire either as a one-off booking or on a regular basis. We aim to make access simple, welcoming and practical for both casual users and organised groups.
