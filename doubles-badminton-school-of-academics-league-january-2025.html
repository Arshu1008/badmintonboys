Welcome to the Life Hope League!
The School of Academics League is an exciting and competitive badminton league for Category C & D players.

This league offers a unique opportunity for eight players to compete in a structured yet flexible environment over an exciting season running from January 2025 to 31 May 2025, with June reserved for any remaining fixtures.

Each player will play three matches against every other player, with matches consisting of the best of three sets played to 21 points with setting. The top two players at the end of the season will earn promotion to the next division.

Flexible availability is required, as we are all working professionals. Matches are scheduled based on player availability, with games typically played on Saturday mornings and afternoons, as well as Wednesday and Thursday evenings. A WhatsApp group is used to coordinate schedules and ensure smooth organisation.

Registration for this season is now closed, but players can stay connected for updates on upcoming leagues and tournaments. Trophies and medals will be awarded to the top-performing players at the end of the season.
