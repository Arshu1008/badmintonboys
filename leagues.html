Badminton Junior Club
Sunday 5pm - 7pm

Welcome to badminton junior club. We are a junior badminton club established over 2 years ago, with over 10 members and qualified Badminton coaches who are DBS checked.

For children aged 8 to 14, we cater for all abilities from beginners to advanced players. Our coaches are trained and promoted from within the club, are registered with Badminton England and are highly experienced.

Our main session takes place every Sunday from 5pm to 7pm at Hamptons Sports and Leisure, Great Baddow, Chelmsford, CM2 9FH. We usually book 2 courts and parents are free to come and watch.

Trial sessions must be arranged in advance. To arrange a trial or register your interest, contact myclub.uk@gmail.com.
