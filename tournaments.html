Tournaments

For the past two years, badminton boys has been organising competitive badminton tournaments for Category C & D players. Our aim is simple: to foster competition, fun and community through affordable, well-run events.

Tournament Overview
Accessible & Competitive

With entry fees as low as £20 per player, our tournaments are designed to be accessible to the badminton community while still offering exciting competition.

Players can compete for trophies and medals in a friendly but challenging environment built around fun, fairness and community spirit.

Where We Play
Chelmsford

  • Hamptons Sports and Leisure
  • Tydemans, off Beehive Ln
  • Great Baddow, Chelmsford CM2 9FH

Who It’s For
Category C & D

Welcome to Badminton boys, the ultimate destination for intermediate-level badminton players who enjoy both the thrill of competition and the fun of social play.

Our tournaments are built for Category C & D players who want more chances to compete, connect and grow their game in a welcoming setting.
